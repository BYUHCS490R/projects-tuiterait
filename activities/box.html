Box Model

1. Box tags can modify the whole "width" of the text and add modification to almos the whole page while going back to the line. The inline tags can only modify the content of the page aka the text without disturbing the whole flow of the website.

2. the spacing in between the text and the box is called the padding. The space outside of the box is called margin. The edge of the box is the border.

3.You can use px, %, em, rem, vw, and vh. Pixels are fixed size and percent or em change with the screen.

4. You use a figure tag when you have an image or diagram with a caption that goes together.
