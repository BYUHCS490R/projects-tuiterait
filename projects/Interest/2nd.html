Che Heart

He's the protagonist of a webtoon called Ordeal (very good would recommend). This world is about many "kymios" (people with superpower) who shares the word with normal human. Because of their huge different in strength there is a separation and different opinion about each other. We follow the story to see which side Che Heart is going to take and it's very interesting because we get glimpse of his futur self and we are left wondering what happened for him to have chose this outcome.

Attributes

Appearence
Blue skin, pointed ears and red eyes.
Powers&Abilities
Powerfull body, Thanatos Talent, Talent Weapon, Martial expert.
Personality&Motivations
Protective Nature, Anti-Hero Role, Very Loyal
